Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping, orders, shipping, and more.

Placing an order on DemoMart is easy! Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can create an account for faster checkout, or checkout as a guest. Follow the steps to enter your shipping information, select a payment method, and complete your order. You'll receive an email confirmation once your order is placed.

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also accept PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through encrypted SSL connections to protect your information.

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (FREE on orders over $50)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($15.99)
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day ($29.99)

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships so you can track its progress.

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Items must be in their original condition with tags attached and original packaging. To return an item:

  1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the item you want to return
  3. Follow the return instructions
  4. Package the item securely and ship it back

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. Free return shipping is available for defective items.
