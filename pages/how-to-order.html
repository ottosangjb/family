How To Order

A simple step-by-step guide for browsing, purchasing, and tracking orders on DemoMart.

1. Browse Products

Use category menus, product filters, and the search experience to find the items you want.

2. Add To Cart

Select your preferred quantity or variation, then add products to your cart before checkout.

3. Checkout Securely

Review your cart, enter shipping details, choose a payment method, and confirm the order.

4. Track Delivery

After purchase, visit the tracking page to check shipping progress and estimated delivery updates.
